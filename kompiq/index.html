Domain 01

Cognitive Style

How you process information, solve problems, and structure your thinking. Are you analytical or intuitive? Detail-focused or big-picture?

Domain 02

What sustains your energy over time versus what depletes you -- even when you're good at it. Capability is not sustainability.

How you share ideas, listen, and persuade. Think-then-speak or think-by-speaking? Direct or diplomatic? Written or verbal?

Domain 04

How you make choices under uncertainty. Fast and reversible, or slow and thorough? Data-driven or gut-driven?

Team Dynamics

Your natural role in groups. Driver, facilitator, challenger, supporter, or specialist? Your team role is not your job title.

Domain 06

Motivation Drivers

What makes you start and sustain effort. Not what you say motivates you, but what your patterns reveal about your actual drivers.

Domain 07

Learning Orientation

How you acquire knowledge and adapt to change. By reading, doing, watching, teaching, or discussing? Generalist or specialist?

Domain 08

Time Relationship

How you plan, prioritize, and relate to deadlines. A planner in a reactive role will implode. This measures your temporal orientation.

Domain 09

Stress Response

How you perform under pressure, what breaks you down, what builds you back up. Your stress wiring shapes your career more than your skills.

Domain 10

Value Alignment

What organizational cultures fit your wiring. You can have the perfect role at the wrong company and still be miserable.

FAQ

Common questions

What does the Kompiq Assessment measure?
The Kompiq Assessment maps your professional DNA across 10 dimensions: Cognitive Style, Energy Patterns, Communication Mode, Decision Architecture, Team Dynamics, Motivation Drivers, Learning Orientation, Time Relationship, Stress Response, and Value Alignment. Together, these reveal the work environments, roles, and teams where you naturally perform at your best.
How long does the assessment take?
18-22 minutes. It consists of 120 forced-choice questions where you choose between two equally valid statements. There are no right or wrong answers. Most people find the format engaging because each question presents a genuine trade-off.
How is this different from MBTI or CliftonStrengths?
MBTI categorizes you into one of 16 types. CliftonStrengths ranks 34 themes. Kompiq doesn't categorize -- it maps 10 continuous dimensions that directly connect to work performance. More importantly, Kompiq measures stress response, value alignment, and environment fit, which neither MBTI nor CliftonStrengths address. And every section of your report is AI-personalized to your specific answer patterns, not template-based.
Is this a clinical or diagnostic assessment?
No. Kompiq is a professional self-insight tool grounded in organizational psychology and career development research. It is not a clinical assessment, does not diagnose any condition, and is not a substitute for professional career counseling or mental health support.
When do I receive my report?
Your report is generated within minutes of completing the assessment. You will receive an email with a link to view your full 40-page report online. You can also save it as a PDF directly from your browser.
Can I share my results?
Yes. Your report includes a "How to Work With [Your Name]" team guide specifically designed to be shared with managers, teammates, or direct reports. It covers your communication preferences, decision-making style, stress signals, and ideal collaboration mode.
What is forced-choice format?
Each question presents two statements that are both positive and valid. You choose which one resonates more on a 5-point scale. This eliminates social desirability bias (you can't pick the "right" answer because both options sound good) and surfaces genuine trade-offs in how you work.
Is there a money-back guarantee?

Methodology

How Your Profile Was Built

The Assessment

Your profile is based on 120 forced-choice questions across 10 career dimensions. Forced-choice methodology reduces social desirability bias: there are no right answers, only honest preferences.

The Scoring

Each answer contributes scores to two traits within its domain. Your domain score is the normalized average of all trait scores, scaled to 0-100. Both ends of every spectrum have professional strengths.

The Archetype

Your archetype is determined by the pattern of your domain scores, not any single dimension. Our system evaluates your profile against defined archetype conditions and selects the best match.

Research Foundations

This assessment draws on Self-Determination Theory, Person-Environment Fit Theory, Cognitive Style Theory, and the Transactional Model of Stress.

This report provides directional guidance based on your responses. Use it as one valuable input among many in your career decisions.

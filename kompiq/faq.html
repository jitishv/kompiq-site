FAQ

Everything you need to know about Kompiq

Answers to the most common questions about the assessment, your report, methodology, and more.

01

About the Assessment

What does the Kompiq Assessment measure?
The Kompiq Assessment maps your professional DNA across 10 dimensions: Cognitive Style, Energy Patterns, Communication Mode, Decision Architecture, Team Dynamics, Motivation Drivers, Learning Orientation, Time Relationship, Stress Response, and Value Alignment. Together, these dimensions reveal the environments, roles, and team structures where you do your best work.
How long does the assessment take?
Most people finish in 18 to 22 minutes. The assessment has 120 forced-choice questions, and there are no right or wrong answers. Just pick the option that feels more like you.
What is forced-choice format?
Each question gives you two statements, and you pick the one that fits you better. This format reduces overthinking and social desirability bias, which means your results reflect how you actually operate rather than how you think you should answer.
Can I pause and come back later?
Yes. Your progress is saved automatically. You can close the browser and return to finish whenever you are ready. For the most accurate results, try to complete it within a day or two so your mindset stays consistent.
Is there a time limit?
No. There is no countdown or timer. Take as long as you need on each question. That said, your first instinct is usually the most accurate, so try not to overthink individual questions.
What devices can I take the assessment on?
The assessment works on any device with a modern browser: desktop, laptop, tablet, or phone. The interface adapts to your screen size. We recommend a distraction-free environment regardless of which device you choose.
02

About the Report

When do I receive my report?
Your personalized report is generated and delivered within 24 hours of completing the assessment. You will receive an email with a link to view and download it.
What sections are included in the report?
Your report includes a full domain analysis across all 10 dimensions, your top 15 ranked strengths, blind spots to watch for, environment fit scoring, roles that match your profile, a stress response profile, a culture fit map, a 90-day growth plan, and a shareable team guide. Each section contains specific, actionable insights.
How is my report personalized?
Every report is generated from scratch using our scoring engine's analysis of your specific response patterns. This is not a template system that slots you into a type. Your report reflects the unique combination of how you scored across all 10 dimensions, producing insights that are specific to you.
Can I share my report with others?
Yes. Your report includes a dedicated shareable team guide designed for managers and colleagues. You can share the full report or just the team guide section. Many professionals share it during onboarding, performance reviews, or team offsites.
Can I save the report as a PDF?
Yes. You can download your full report as a PDF directly from the report page. The PDF is formatted for easy reading and printing, so you can keep a copy offline or share it as a file.
How long is the report?
Reports typically run 25 to 35 pages depending on the complexity of your profile. Every section is written in plain language with specific recommendations, not filler content or generic advice.
03

Comparison and Positioning

How is Kompiq different from MBTI?
MBTI assigns you one of 16 personality types. Kompiq does not type you at all. Instead, it measures 10 work-specific dimensions on a spectrum, giving you a detailed profile rather than a label. Kompiq also focuses on career application: role fit, environment preferences, and growth strategies rather than general personality descriptions.
How is Kompiq different from CliftonStrengths?
CliftonStrengths identifies your top talent themes from a fixed list of 34. Kompiq takes a different approach by mapping how you work across 10 interconnected dimensions, then showing how those patterns affect your career fit. You get role recommendations, environment matching, and a growth plan rather than just a ranked list of strengths.
How is this different from free career quizzes?
Free quizzes typically use 10 to 20 simple questions and produce generic one-page results. Kompiq uses 120 forced-choice questions across 10 validated dimensions and generates a 25+ page personalized report specific to your exact profile. The depth of measurement and specificity of output are not comparable.
Is this better for career changers or people in existing roles?
Both. If you are considering a career change, the role fit and environment sections help you target the right next move. If you are staying in your current role, the blind spots, stress profile, and 90-day growth plan help you perform better where you are. The report is designed to be useful regardless of where you are in your career.
Can teams use Kompiq?
Yes. Each team member takes the assessment individually and receives their own report with a shareable team guide. Managers use these to understand communication preferences, decision styles, and stress responses across the team. For team purchases, contact us at hello@kompiq.com.
04

Science and Methodology

What research is the Kompiq Assessment based on?
Kompiq draws from established frameworks in organizational psychology, including research on cognitive styles, work motivation, decision-making patterns, and person-environment fit. The 10 dimensions were selected based on the factors that most strongly predict career satisfaction and performance in applied research.
Is this a clinical or diagnostic assessment?
No. Kompiq is a professional self-insight tool. It is not a clinical instrument and does not diagnose any psychological or medical condition. It is designed to help you make better career decisions and understand your work patterns, not to replace professional counseling.
How accurate is the Kompiq Assessment?
The forced-choice format is specifically designed to reduce response bias and social desirability effects, which are the two biggest threats to assessment accuracy. Your results reflect your actual tendencies, not what you think sounds best. For the most accurate results, answer quickly and honestly.
What methodology does Kompiq use?
Kompiq uses a forced-choice paired-comparison format across 120 questions. Your responses are scored across 10 dimensions using a weighted algorithm, then analyzed by our scoring engine to generate a personalized report. The methodology prioritizes behavioral tendencies over self-reported preferences, which produces more reliable career insights.

Yes, though we recommend waiting at least 6 months between attempts. Your core work patterns tend to be stable over time. If you retake it too soon, you may unconsciously try to reproduce or change your previous answers, which reduces accuracy.
